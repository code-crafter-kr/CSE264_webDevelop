Bio

Early Life

As a typical South Korean high school student, I embarked on an unconventional educational path by studying at a local university, then as an exchange student in the U.S., followed by transferring to a community college and finally to Lehigh University. This unplanned journey into studying abroad turned out to be a remarkable success.

Life at Lehigh

Starting with challenging dorm life, I've adapted to a more fulfilling off-campus lifestyle. Impressed by the quality of Lehigh's coursework, my academic journey has had its ups and downs, pushing me to strive harder in my current studies after a disappointing second semester.

Future Life

Post-graduation, I plan to return to South Korea with aspirations not of joining the conventional job market, but to pursue my passion for teaching coding. My ultimate dream is to establish a renowned academy, sharing my knowledge and making a meaningful impact.
